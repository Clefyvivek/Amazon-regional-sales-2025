# Amazon-regional-sales-2025

## Executive Summary: 
- Sales performance reached **$3.11M across 2,500 orders**, with an average order value of **$1.24K**. Growth momentum is strong, with **8.94% MoM growth** and clear seasonal peaks in **June (809 orders)** and **November (+21.37% growth)**.
 
- **Electronics remain the dominant category (3,500 units sold)**, but regional differences highlight strategic opportunities: **Dallas leads in revenue ($360K) due to home appliances**, while **New York tops in order volume (285 orders)**. Miami’s rapid growth in Q2 signals an emerging market.
 
- The data underscores the importance of **seasonal promotions, regional product tailoring, and category diversification** to sustain growth and maximize profitability


### Goals:

### Key Takeaway: </br>
Sales growth is driven by **seasonal demand spikes, electronics dominance, and regional product preferences**. To sustain momentum, the business must **leverage holiday promotions, expand appliance offerings in high‑value regions, and strengthen emerging markets like Miami.**

### Data & Modeling:

### Recommendations:
- **Seasonal Promotions**: Intensify marketing and discount strategies in **Q2 and November**, aligning with peak demand cycles.
- **Regional Product Strategy**: Expand **home appliance offerings in Dallas** and replicate this mix in similar high‑value markets.
- **Category Focus**: Maintain strong **electronics inventory** while diversifying into complementary categories (clothing, appliances) to capture holiday demand.
- **Market Expansion**: Target **Miami** with tailored campaigns, leveraging its rapid Q2 growth in electronics and appliances.
